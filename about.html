History

The enchanting tale of CozyCup Cafe began in 2023, when two people passionate feline enthusiasts decided to create a unique space that combined their love for coffee and cats. Inspired by the flourishing cat cafe trend from around the world, they opened the doors to a haven where patrons could sip on their favorite brews and taking a bite to pastry while basking in the delightful company of furry companions.



Mission

At our café, we are dedicated to crafting a cozy and welcoming haven for coffee enthusiasts and cat lovers alike. We blend the comforting aroma of freshly brewed coffee with the playful purrs of resident feline companions, creating an environment where every sip and every purr inspire a sense of belonging and tranquility.

Values and Commmitment to Quality

CozyCup Cafe was founded with a strong love for both two-legged and four-legged customers. The café appreciates the joy and company cats offer to people's lives. This setting exudes warmth and happiness as the calming purrs of the local cats blend in perfect harmony with the scent of freshly made coffee and pastry.

CozyCup Cafe with Cats ensures that both its coffee offerings and its feline residents are of the highest quality. The coffee menu boasts an array of artisanal blends and brews sourced from ethical suppliers, guaranteeing an exceptional coffee experience. Equally important, the cafe's feline residents are chosen for their gentle nature and friendly demeanor, creating an environment that is enjoyable for both cat lovers and newcomers alike.
